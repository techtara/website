About

From the earliest days of computer science, women have made crucial contributions to its progress. As our world today moves towards a brighter and more equal future, it is time we sit up and take notice of these contributions. Organizations around the globe are pushing for more recognition for women in technology, and young girls are being encouraged to take up technology as a career choice. In keeping with this spirit, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology is proud to host its first ever event focused on the role of women in technology on 6th and 7th March. Organized with the theme “Celebrate, Inspire, Network”, TechTara will feature a diverse and eminent panel of speakers, who will deliver talks and participate in panel discussions. Other events will also be conducted to help students identify and display their skills.

Speakers

Smt D. Sandhya Sree Reddy

Smt D. Sandhya Sree Reddy, chairperson D&P Committee, CBES, did her Bachelor of Architecture in JNTU, Hyderabad and completed Masters in Architecture in School of Architecture and Planning, New Delhi.

She is a practicing architect in Hyderabad since 1990. She is a member of Indian Institute of Architects and a member of International Society of Landscape Architecture.

Dr.Priti Chandra

As the Head of Cyber Security Egovernance & Strategic Systems at ASL, Dr.Priti Chandra has been serving DRDO for the last 25 years. She is a Computer Science Post Graduate and was awarded Doctorate by Central University, Hyderabad for her research in AI.

Her vast experience on DRDO systems for armed forces includes Integrated Security Surveillance systems, camouflaging for the strategic missile program. She has published 25 research papers in International Journals and conferences. She is a regular reviewer in International Journals and Conferences.

Pravallika Achythuni

Pravallika Achyuthuni is the Vice President of Swecha , a non-profit organisation which works to take forward free software and its ideological implications to all corners of our country from the developed domains to the underprivileged. She has been associated with the organisation for 10 years now.

She has done her post graduation in Business Analytics from Great Lakes Institute of Management. She has 5+ years of experience as a Main Frame developer and is currently working at Verizon India.

Pratyusha Parakala

A software professional turned RJ, Pratyusha Parakala is the only woman from AP and Telangana to go on an expedition to Antarctica to study about climatic change and sustainable living. She was the Climate Force Antarctica Ambassador in 2018.

She received state-level Vishista Puraskaram Award, 2018 from the Government of Telangana. She is also an expert in applying academic background and technical skills to create a positive drift in the organisation. She even launched a new radio station, Mirchi Love in Hyderabad.

Aziz Tayyaba

Aziz Unnisa Tayyaba, former Indian Airforce officer,has been a “hard-core" air warrior so much so that her parasailing exploits were even listed in the Limca Book of Records. In 2009, after 16 years in the air force, she completed her commission and was forced to retire.

She went to the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad, completed the six-month business management course for defence personnel and joined the human resources department of the Shapoorji Pallonji Group in Hyderabad. She tries to use her experience to add new dimensions to her current workplace.
